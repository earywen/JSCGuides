# Chimaerus the Undreamt God  Heroic Boss Guide

![Chimaerus the Undreamt God](https://www.method.gg/images/raidrace/bosses/the-dreamrift/chimaerustheundreamtgod.png)

[Heroic](https://www.method.gg/guides/the-dreamrift/chimaerus-the-undreamt-god-heroic) Mythic

Purke

**Last Updated:** 2nd Mar, 2026

## Raid Setup

![tank icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/tank-icon.png)

2 Tanks

![healer icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/healer-icon.png)

4 Healers

![dps icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/dps-icon.png)

14 DPS

## Introduction

Welcome to our Heroic Chimaerus, the Undreamt God guide for The Dreamrift Raid. This guide covers the fight mechanics and the strategy we recommend to defeat this one boss raid on Heroic difficulty.

This is the “one boss raid” in Midnight Season 1, with the intended difficulty to be similar to a middle boss of The Voidspire. It’s one of those encounters that will test your raid groups communication skills and patience. “Swap.”, “No, not that.”, “Who has the thing?”, “Why is that add immune?” or “Stop feeding him!”. It’s chaos, but the fun kind, once people stop standing around admiring the scenery.

**Fight Style**: Single target boss with heavy add pressure and frequent target swaps

**Recommended Setup**: 2x Tank / 4x Healer / 14x DPS

**Required utilities**: None

**Hero/Lust usage**: On Pull

## Method Boss Review

If we ignore the excessive amount of trash you have to kill just to reach this boss in a one boss raid instance, this is a pretty solid early-tier encounter.

There aren’t any brand new, crazy-unique mechanics, but the overall flow feels good and the fight stays busy without being obnoxious. The Mythic version does a lot of heavy lifting by making it more challenging and interesting, which is why this lands as an overall “good” boss.

Method Boss Rating:  (3/5)

## Easy Mode

**General:**

- Hero/Lust on pull.
- Before you pull, split the raid into two even groups:
  - Group A: 1 tank, 2 healers, 7 DPS
  - Group B: 1 tank, 2 healers, 7 DPS
- First [~~~~Alndust Upheaval](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1262289/alndust-upheaval?dd=15) happens early:

  - Put the circle off to the side.
  - The assigned group soaks it together to get [~~~~Alnsight](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245698/alnsight?dd=15).
- While the boss phase is going:
  - **[~~~~Alnsight](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245698/alnsight?dd=15) group** goes down, follows their tank, and starts on the **big add**.
  - Let the small adds stack up near the big add so you can AoE them together.
  - Kick and dispel **Haunting Essence** casts so the raid doesn’t fall apart.
  - **Normal group** starts on the boss, then swaps to adds the moment they become properly killable.
- [~~~~Consuming Miasma](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1257087/consuming-miasma?dd=15)players:

  - Move to a puddle, call for dispel, and clip a puddle with your circle to clear it.
  - Do not dispel in the raid, it splashes damage.
- [~~~~Rending Tear](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1272726/rending-tear?dd=15) happens: If it’s aimed at you, step out. Don’t donate a bleed to the group.
- Biggest rule of the fight: Do not let adds reach the boss. One mistake can snowball fast.
- At **100 energy**, the boss casts [~~~~Consume](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245396/consume?dd=15): The Knockback happens, then intermission starts.

**Intermission:**

- Dodge the fly-through line ( [~~~~Corrupted Devastation](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245486/corrupted-devastation?dd=15)) and don’t stand in the puddles it leaves.
- Kill the Manifestations and keep clearing puddles with the Miasma dispels.
- Make sure all adds are dead before [~~~~Ravenous Dive](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245406/ravenous-dive?dd=15), or they get eaten.

Then you’re back to Phase 1 and repeat the same loop until the boss falls over.

## Mechanics

Expand for details

Boss

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1262289/alndust-upheaval?dd=15)![Alndust Upheaval Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/ability_earthen_azeritesurge.jpg)

### Alndust Upheaval

The boss targets the current tank, dealing heavy Nature damage split between players standing in the circle.

Players that get hit will be knocked up and gain [~~~~Alnsight](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245698/alnsight?dd=15), which lets them attack Manifestations for 40 seconds. When [~~~~Alnsight](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245698/alnsight?dd=15) expires, players get [~~~~Rift Vulnerability](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1253744/rift-vulnerability?dd=15), causing them to take heavy damage from [~~~~Alndust Upheaval](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1262289/alndust-upheaval?dd=15) for 1.5 minutes. This forces you to alternate soak groups.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1258610/rift-emergence?dd=15)![Rift Emergence Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_azeritefireball.jpg)

### Rift Emergence

Spawns Manifestations and hits the entire raid with an absorb called [~~~~Rift Sickness](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1250953/rift-sickness?dd=15).

It also applies [~~~~Alnshroud](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245727/alnshroud?dd=15) to the adds as an absorb shield. Breaking [~~~~Alnshroud](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245727/alnshroud?dd=15) makes Manifestations targetable by players without Alndust effects as well.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245919/alndust-essence?dd=15)![Alndust Essence Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv__azerite-area-denial.jpg)

### Alndust Essence

Each add that dies leaves behind a puddle that: Slows players by 50% and deals damage over time if you stand in it.

![Colossal Horror, Haunting Essence and Swarming Shade Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_shadow_deathscream.jpg)

### Colossal Horror, Haunting Essence and Swarming Shade

**Colossal Horror (Tank add)** \- Needs to be tanked. It just hits harder over time with each strike.

**Haunting Essence** \- Casts [~~~~Fearsome Cry](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249017/fearsome-cry?dd=15), which fears several players. This can be dispelled and interrupted. It also casts [~~~~Essence Bolt](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1261997/essence-bolt?dd=15), flat damage on a player. This can be interrupted too.

**Swarming Shade** \- No real abilities. Their entire job is to reach the boss and ruin your night.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1252863/insatiable?dd=15)![Insatiable Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_shadow_lifedrain.jpg)

### Insatiable

The boss eats any Manifestation that reaches him, which:

- Deals raid-wide damage
- Heals the boss for 200% of the Manifestation’s missing health
- Increases the boss’s damage done by 100%

This stacks, so if multiple adds reach the boss, you’re usually wiping immediately.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1257087/consuming-miasma?dd=15)![Consuming Miasma Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_azerite_essence_16.jpg)

### Consuming Miasma

Several players get a large circle that deals damage every 1.5 seconds for 1 minute unless dispelled.

When dispelled, it clears puddles left behind from adds if the player is standing in them. It also deals damage to players nearby on removal, so don’t stack when you’re getting dispelled.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1246653/caustic-phlegm?dd=15)![Caustic Phlegm Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_ability_poison_orb.jpg)

### Caustic Phlegm

A raid-wide damage over time effect lasting 12 seconds. Healers need to pay attention here, especially during overlaps.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1272726/rending-tear?dd=15)![Rending Tear Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_druid_bloodythrash.jpg)

### Rending Tear

A frontal cone targeted at a random player. Deals heavy damage and applies a bleed to anyone hit. This should be dodged.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245396/consume?dd=15)![Consume Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_deathknight_gnaw_ghoul.jpg)

### Consume

At 100 energy, the boss consumes any remaining Manifestations, even if they are not at the boss yet.

This knocks everyone away and starts the intermission.

Intermission

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245486/corrupted-devastation?dd=15)![Corrupted Devastation Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_azerite_essence02.jpg)

### Corrupted Devastation

Breath-style mechanic where the boss flies through the area in a line, dealing massive damage and stunning anyone hit.

This also spawns Manifestations and leaves behind a puddle.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1246132/rift-shroud?dd=15)![Rift Shroud Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_nature_elementalshields.jpg)

### Rift Shroud

The boss takes 99% reduced damage during this phase.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245406/ravenous-dive?dd=15)![Ravenous Dive Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/ability_earthen_pillar.jpg)

### Ravenous Dive

The boss crashes into the ground, knocks everyone up, and consumes any remaining Manifestations, then triggers Phase 1 again.

## Strategy

This encounter is mechanically simple, but it really punishes making mistakes.

Before you begin, split the raid into two equal groups, each with: 1 Tank, 2 Healers and 7 DPS.

## Phase 1

Shortly after you pull the boss, you will get your first [~~~~Alndust Upheaval](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1262289/alndust-upheaval?dd=15) cast. Place the circle off to the side, so the assigned group can soak it cleanly.

![chimaerus phase 1 alndust upheaval](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/0103395-chimaerus-phase-1-alndust-upheaval.jpg)

From here, you basically have two groups doing different jobs.

### Alndust Group

As you go down, run toward the big add with your tank and start on it first.

Do not touch the small adds yet; you want them to naturally group up near the big add so you can kill everything when they are stacked. The small adds start moving to the boss after you break their absorb shield, so opening on the big add helps keep the pack together as the shields fall off.

Several players should handle interrupts on **Haunting Essence**.

### Normal Group

This group mainly attacks the boss at the start.

Once several players get [~~~~Consuming Miasma](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1257087/consuming-miasma?dd=15), move to a puddle and call for a dispel. All it takes is your circle barely touching the puddle to remove it, so you don’t need to stand in the middle of it.

![chimaerus consuming miasma](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/0103392-chimaerus-consuming-miasma.jpg)

Some players will get targeted by the frontal. Just dodge it.

Eventually, adds will become targetable in the normal world. At that point, everyone swaps and kills them fast, because even one add reaching the boss can be lethal.

Your goal is to kill all of the adds before the next group has to go down. When the next [~~~~Alndust Upheaval](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1262289/alndust-upheaval?dd=15) happens, you repeat the process.

The boss will eventually hit 100 energy and cast [~~~~Consume](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245396/consume?dd=15), eating any remaining adds even if they aren’t at the boss yet. Everyone gets knocked away and the intermission starts.

## Intermission

This is a simple, repetitive phase.

The boss places a large line that he flies through, spawning Manifestations and leaving puddles.

![chimaerus intermission](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/0103393-chimaerus-intermission.jpg)

The raid kills Manifestations and removes puddles using the dispel mechanic from Phase 1.

Eventually the boss casts [~~~~Ravenous Dive](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245406/ravenous-dive?dd=15) and slams into the ground. Make sure that all adds are dead before this happens, otherwise they get consumed.

Then the fight restarts and you do exactly the same thing again until the boss dies.
