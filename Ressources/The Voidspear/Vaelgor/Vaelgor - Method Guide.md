# Vaelgor & Ezzorak  Heroic Boss Guide

![Vaelgor & Ezzorak](https://www.method.gg/images/raidrace/bosses/the-voidspire/vaelgorezzorak.png)

[Heroic](https://www.method.gg/guides/the-voidspire/vaelgor-ezzorak-heroic) Mythic

Purke

**Last Updated:** 8th Mar, 2026

## Raid Setup

![tank icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/tank-icon.png)

2 Tanks

![healer icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/healer-icon.png)

4 Healers

![dps icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/dps-icon.png)

14 DPS

## Introduction

Welcome to our Heroic Vaelgor & Ezzorak guide for The Voidspire. This fight is a full nostalgia trip, because it’s a double dragon encounter, which means all the boomers in your raid finally get their moment to go “back in my day, every dragon had a Tail Lash”.

And honestly, they’re not wrong. If you stand behind either of these two for even a second, you’re getting launched, and you will deserve it.

**Fight Style:** Two-Target Fight (Multi DoT)

**Recommended Setup:** 2x Tank / 4x Healer / 14x DPS

**Required utilities:** None

**Hero/Lust usage:** On Pull

## Method Boss Review

This fight has some genuinely cool ideas, especially the whole [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) shrinking mechanic, but there are also a couple things that make the experience a bit worse than it needs to be.

The big one is how much it feels like it requires a Mass Grip to stack the Voidorbs for AoE. You can play without it, sure, but it definitely feels like the fight is silently judging your comp the whole time.

And then there’s the intermission, which is… a glowing pancake in the middle of the room. It’s not doing much besides glowing. I also don’t love how severe the punishment is for a 10% HP difference, even on Heroic. It’s fine early on, but once people get geared and damage starts getting uneven, that part is going to become pretty annoying.

Method Boss Rating:  (3/5)

## Easy Mode

**General:**

- Two bosses, not shared health. Split damage so they die at the same speed.
- Keep them **more than 15 yards apart** and within **10% HP**, or [~~~~Twilight Bond](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1270189/twilight-bond?dd=15) gives them 100% extra damage until fixed.
- Tank them near where they spawn, close to the 15-yard limit for cleave value.
- Raid stands between them and hits from the sides. Do not stand behind either boss because [~~~~Tail Lash](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1264467/tail-lash?dd=15) will launch you.

**Dread Breath:**

- The targeted player with [~~~~Dread Breath](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244221/dread-breath?dd=15) steps to the side so the cone doesn’t hit the raid.
- Get dispelled after it hits.

**Main Repeating Combo:**

1. [~~~~Void Howl](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244917/void-howl?dd=15)
2. [~~~~Nullbeam](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1262623/nullbeam?dd=15) / [~~~~Nullzone](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244672/nullzone?dd=15)
3. [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) about 10 seconds later

This always repeats in the same order.

**Void Howl + Voidorbs:**

- During [~~~~Void Howl](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244917/void-howl?dd=15), stack as tight as possible without overlapping circles so the orbs spawn stacked.
- When [~~~~Nullzone](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244672/nullzone?dd=15) tethers everyone: Everyone snaps their tether right away and the Tank snaps last, because the last snap triggers the raid DoT.
- Mass Grip the orbs if you have it, stun them, and AoE them down fast so [~~~~Voidbolt](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245175/voidbolt?dd=15) doesn’t free-cast.

**Gloom:**

- Aim the first [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) to the **blue marker**.
- Have **5 ranged players** soak it on the way to shrink the puddle.
- Rotate markers and rotate soak teams each time, because [~~~~Diminish](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1270852/diminish?dd=15) lasts 1 minute and stops you soaking twice.

**Tank Swaps:**

- Swap bosses after each [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15). Just run over to the other boss, don’t drag them around.

**Intermission:**

- At 100 energy, stack in the [~~~~Radiant Barrier](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1248847/radiant-barrier?dd=15) for [~~~~Midnight Flames](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249748/midnight-flames?dd=15).
- Players with purple circles move out to the side.
- Kill **Manifestation of Midnight** fast so it doesn’t ramp.
- After intermission, put bosses back to the same spots and repeat until dead.

## Mechanics

Expand for details

General

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1270189/twilight-bond?dd=15)![Twilight Bond Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_120_raid_voidspire_dragonduo.jpg)

### Twilight Bond

If the bosses are within 15 yards of each other, or if there is a 10% HP difference between them, they gain a 100% damage done buff until the issue is fixed.

So keep them separated and keep their health roughly even, or the raid gets punished immediately.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249748/midnight-flames?dd=15)![Midnight Flames Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_cosmicdragonmount.jpg)

### Midnight Flames

At 100 energy, both bosses fly into the sky and start dealing heavy damage to anyone not standing inside the [~~~~Radiant Barrier](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1248847/radiant-barrier?dd=15).

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1248847/radiant-barrier?dd=15)![Radiant Barrier Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_holy_blessedresillience.jpg)

### Radiant Barrier

Spawns in the middle of the room during the intermission and protects players from the unavoidable [~~~~Midnight Flames](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249748/midnight-flames?dd=15) damage.

![Manifestation of Midnight Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_nzinsanity_chasedbyshadows.jpg)

### Manifestation of Midnight

This add spawns during the intermission and targets players with a circle that forces them to move out to the side so they don’t clip others.

Every 30 seconds that it stays alive, it gains: 75% increased attack speed and 50% reduced effectiveness of slowing effects.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1280458/grappling-maw?dd=15)![Grappling Maw Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/ability_racial_flayer.jpg)

### Grappling Maw

![tank icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/tank-icon.png)

The dragon that is currently flying, grips their current target, dealing damage based on distance. The further away the target is, the more damage it does.

The bosses alternate flying stance after each intermission.

Vaelgor

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1262623/nullbeam?dd=15)![Nullbeam Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/ability_ironmaidens_convulsiveshadows.jpg)

### Nullbeam

![tank icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/tank-icon.png)

A frontal beam on the current tank that deals damage for 4 seconds and applies a stacking DoT to everyone hit.

The tank wants to soak enough stacks to reduce the pull strength of [~~~~Nullzone](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244672/nullzone?dd=15), usually aiming for around 8 (max) stacks before stepping out.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244672/nullzone?dd=15)![Nullzone Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/warlock_curse_shadow.jpg)

### Nullzone

Spawns at the location where the tank soaked [~~~~Nullbeam](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1262623/nullbeam?dd=15). It tethers every player and slowly pulls them in.

Players need to snap their tethers. The tank should be the last one to snap, because the final snap triggers [~~~~Nullzone Implosion](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1252157/nullzone-implosion?dd=15), which applies a heavy raid-wide DoT for 6 seconds.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244221/dread-breath?dd=15)![Dread Breath Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/ability_rogue_envelopingshadows.jpg)

### Dread Breath

Targets a random player with a large frontal cone and fears anyone hit.

Move the cone out to the side. The targeted player is guaranteed to get feared, so plan to get them dispelled after the hit

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265131/vaelwing?dd=15)![Vaelwing Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_icon_wing07c.jpg)

### Vaelwing

![tank icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/tank-icon.png)

A physical tank hit that ramps up in strength with each strike, and resets when the boss hits a new target.

Also, players standing behind the boss get hit by [~~~~Tail Lash](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1264467/tail-lash?dd=15), which knocks them back and applies a bleed.

Ezzorak

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15)![Gloom Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_cosmicvoid_nova.jpg)

### Gloom

![tank icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/tank-icon.png)

Fires a large orb in the direction Ezzorak is facing. When it reaches the edge of the area, it explodes and leaves a massive puddle.

The puddle shrinks based on how many players touch the [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) on the way, up to a maximum of 5.

Players who touch [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) gain a 1-minute debuff called [~~~~Diminish](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1270852/diminish?dd=15). If they touch [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) again while they have it, they take massive damage. Alternate groups for soaking.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244917/void-howl?dd=15)![Void Howl Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_cosmicvoid_orb.jpg)

### Void Howl

Marks all players with a circle that, after a short delay, deals damage to anyone standing in it and spawns a **Voidorb**.

Voidorbs permanently cast [~~~~Voidbolt](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245175/voidbolt?dd=15) at random players. This can be interrupted.

You want to group up the Voidorbs and stun plus AoE them down quickly. This always happens together with [~~~~Nullbeam](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1262623/nullbeam?dd=15).

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245645/rakfang?dd=15)![Rakfang Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_misc_rubysanctum4.jpg)

### Rakfang

![tank icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/tank-icon.png)

A massive physical hit that, after each strike, applies a healing absorb to the tank. This ramps up and resets when it hits a new target.

After each [~~~~Rakfang](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245645/rakfang?dd=15), the boss immediately casts [~~~~Impale](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1265152/impale?dd=15), a cone-shaped [~~~~Tail Lash](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1264467/tail-lash?dd=15) that applies a bleed and stuns players hit for 3 seconds.

## Strategy

This is mainly a one-phase fight with a short intermission. It’s a two-target encounter with big AoE burst moments. The bosses do not share HP, so based on your comp you want to split damage fairly evenly to make sure both bosses die at roughly the same time.

Place the markers like shown here:

![vaelgor ezzorak markers and positioning](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/0103169-vaelgor-ezzorak-markers-and-positioning.jpg)

You want to tank the bosses pretty much where they stand, close to the 15-yard limit. That keeps them separated so [~~~~Twilight Bond](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1270189/twilight-bond?dd=15) doesn’t happen, but still close enough that cleave and multi-dot value is good. The bosses don’t really move, so keeping them locked in place is easy.

The raid should stand between the two bosses, attacking from the sides. Standing behind a boss is not allowed. [~~~~Tail Lash](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1264467/tail-lash?dd=15) is still a thing, and it still hurts.

### Always Watch Boss HP

Make sure the bosses stay within 10% HP of each other, or [~~~~Twilight Bond](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1270189/twilight-bond?dd=15) gives them a 100% damage buff until you fix it.

### Dread Breath

If you get targeted by [~~~~Dread Breath](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244221/dread-breath?dd=15), step out to the side so you don’t clip the raid, then get dispelled after it hits.

![vaelgor ezzorak dreadbreath](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/0103162-vaelgor-ezzorak-dreadbreath.jpg)

### The Main Repeating Combo

The fight has a consistent combo that repeats in the same order:

1. [~~~~Void Howl](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244917/void-howl?dd=15) on all players (spawns Voidorbs)
2. [~~~~Nullbeam](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1262623/nullbeam?dd=15) on the tank (spawns [~~~~Nullzone](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244672/nullzone?dd=15) and tethers everyone)
3. [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) about 10 seconds later

### Void Howl + Voidorbs

During [~~~~Void Howl](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1244917/void-howl?dd=15), everyone should stack as close together as possible without overlapping circles, so the orbs spawn as stacked as you can make them.

![vaelgor ezzorak voidhowl orbs](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/0103166-vaelgor-ezzorak-voidhowl-orbs.jpg)

As soon as the orbs spawn:

- Everyone breaks their tether immediately, by moving away from the anchor, except the tank.
- The tank breaks last, because the final snap triggers the raid DoT.

Right after the raid breaks:

- Use a DK Mass Grip if you have it, or any other grips.
- Press every AoE button in the game.
- Stun the orbs to stop [~~~~Voidbolt](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245175/voidbolt?dd=15) casts.
- Once the raid is stable, the tank snaps last.

### Gloom Handling

Now you get [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15).

Aim the first [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) toward the **blue marker**, and have at least 5 ranged players touch it on the way to shrink the puddle as much as possible.

![vaelgor ezzorak gloom](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/0103160-vaelgor-ezzorak-gloom.jpg)

For each following [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15), rotate to the next marker and have a different set of 5 people stand in its path. You need different people to soak, because anyone who soaks gets [~~~~Diminish](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1270852/diminish?dd=15) for 1 minute and cannot safely soak again until it's gone.

You should get two [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) casts before the intermission.

### Tank Swaps

Tanks should swap bosses after each [~~~~Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=15) cast. Don’t drag the bosses around, just run over and pick up the other one.

Also, pay attention to which boss is flying during that phase. The boss that is flying has an additional ability called [~~~~Grappling Maw](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1280458/grappling-maw?dd=15) which grips the tank and deals damage based on the distance. Their flying stance swaps after each Intermission.

## Intermission

At 100 energy, both bosses fly up and cast [~~~~Midnight Flames](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249748/midnight-flames?dd=15).

Everyone stacks in the middle, inside the [~~~~Radiant Barrier](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1248847/radiant-barrier?dd=15), and anyone with the purple circle moves out to the side so they don’t clip others.

![vaelgor ezzorak intermission radiant barrier](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/0103167-vaelgor-ezzorak-intermission-radiant-barrier.jpg)

A **Manifestation of Midnight** spawns during the intermission. Kill it quickly. If it stays alive too long, it ramps up attack speed every 30 seconds and slows stop working as well, which turns it into a real problem.

Once the intermission ends and the bosses land, put them back in the same tanking spots and repeat the fight from the start.
