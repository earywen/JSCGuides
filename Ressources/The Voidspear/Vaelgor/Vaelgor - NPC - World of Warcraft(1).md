-   **Overview** – The dragon siblings begin battle alternating flight and grounded combat, swapping after [\[Midnight Flames\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249748/midnight-flames?dd=16) at full power. In response the Lightbound Vanguard aid players providing their [\[Radiant Barrier\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1248847/radiant-barrier?dd=16) to nearby players.  
    Eventually both dragons land combining their draconic might to annihilate players once and for all.
    
    -   ![Tank](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-tank.png "Tank")**Tanks** –  
        
        Both [\[Nullbeam\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1262623/nullbeam?dd=16) and [\[Gloom\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=16) orient in the direction of the dragon's current target.
        
          
        [\[Rakfang\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245645/rakfang?dd=16) inflicts Shadow damage and [\[Vaelwing\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265131/vaelwing?dd=16) inflicts Physical damage multiple times based on how far away the dragon's current target is.  
        Receiving [\[Nullbeam\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1262623/nullbeam?dd=16) stacks decrease the [\[Nullzone\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244672/nullzone?dd=16) pull magnitude for all players.
        
    

**Draconic Duo**

-   [Vaelgor](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=250892/vaelgor)
    
    -   ![Magic](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-magic.png "Magic")[Dread Breath](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244221/dread-breath?dd=16) – Vaelgor roars from the darkest void towards a targeted player, fearing enemies in a massive frontal cone. This exhalation inflicts 233495 Shadow damage plus an additional 116551 Shadow damage every 3 sec, increasing movement speed by 50%, and fears for 21 sec.
        
    -   ![Important](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-important.png "Important")[Nullbeam](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1262623/nullbeam?dd=16) – Vaelgor expels crystalline spacetime for 4 sec inflicting 11655 Shadow damage every 0.5 sec for 8 sec to players in a frontal direction. This effect stacks.  
        Upon completion the lattice shatters forming a Nullzone, tethering all players. Nullzone's pull magnitude weakens as Nullbeam stacks, up to 12 times.
        
        -   [Nullzone](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244672/nullzone?dd=16) – Forms an unstable rift which tethers players, pulling inward inflicting 11655 Shadow damage every 1 sec. This effect stacks.  
            Stretching this tether an additional 10 yards beyond the initial radius triggers a Nullsnap. If no other tethered players remain the field collapses triggering a Nullzone Implosion instead.
            
            -   [Nullsnap](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244413/nullsnap?dd=16) – The cosmic bindings shatter inflicting 11655 Shadow damage to all players.
                
            -   [Nullzone Implosion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1252157/nullzone-implosion?dd=16) – The cosmic field collapses inflicting 77701 Shadow damage and an additional 27196 Shadow damage every 0.5 sec for 9 sec to all players.
                
            -   ![Mythic](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-mythic.png "Mythic")[Nullscatter](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1266570/nullscatter?dd=16) – Nullzone's first tether snap releases a cascade of cosmic hail inflicting 233103 Shadow damage to players within 5 yards of each impact location.
                
            
        
    -   ![Tank](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-tank.png "Tank")[Vaelwing](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265131/vaelwing?dd=16) – While grounded Vaelgor buffets their primary target knocking them back and inflicting 291379 Shadow damage and 194253 Physical damage.  
        This empowers the caster causing melee attacks to additionally inflict 38850 Shadow damage. This effect stacks, until Vaelwing strikes a new target, resetting to a single stack.
        
        -   [Tail Lash](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1264467/tail-lash?dd=16) – Vaelgor savages targets within a 35 yard rear cone bleeding for 77701 Physical damage plus an additional 27195 Physical damage every 0.5 sec for 4 sec, knocking them back.  
            This occurs immediately after casting Vaelwing.
            
        
    
-   [Ezzorak](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=254109/ezzorak)
    
    -   [Void Howl](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244917/void-howl?dd=16) – Ezzorak howls in utter shadow inflicting 155402 Shadow damage to players within 5 yards of indicated players.  
        Summons a Voidorb at targeted players' locations which cast Voidbolt until defeated.
        
        -   ![Interruptible](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-interrupt.png "Interruptible")[Voidbolt](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245175/voidbolt?dd=16) – The Voidorb ejects raw void magic from its core inflicting 38831 Shadow damage plus an additional 11649 Shadow damage every 1 sec for 5 sec. This effect stacks.
            
        
    -   ![Important](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-important.png "Important")[Gloom](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245391/gloom?dd=16) – Ezzorak ejects a moving mass of pure darkness in a frontal direction inflicting 135977 to (135977 \* 3) Shadow damage to all players upon arrival, creating a Gloomfield.  
        Gloom damage done and the size of Gloomfield decrease up to 8 times as players contact the darkness, applying Gloomtouched.
        
        -   [Gloomtouched](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245554/gloomtouched?dd=16) – A dark malady drips from those in contact with Gloom inflicting 77701 Shadow damage plus an additional 58276 Shadow damage every 3 sec for 12 sec to players within 0 yards of the contacting player. This effect stacks.  
            Upon removal Gloomtouched applies Diminish.
            
            On this difficulty, Gloomtouched applies within a radius upon contact.
            
            -   ![Heroic](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-heroic.png "Heroic")[Diminish](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1270852/diminish?dd=16) – Gloomtouched's affliction weighs heavily on targets increasing damage taken by Gloomtouched by 1000% for 1.5 min. This effect stacks.
                
            
        -   [Gloomfield](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245420/gloomfield?dd=16) – Galactic emptiness engulfs a massive location in darkness for 1.8 min inflicting 38850 Shadow damage every 0.5 sec and reducing movement speed by 75%.
            
        
    -   ![Tank](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-tank.png "Tank")[Rakfang](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245645/rakfang?dd=16) – While grounded Ezzorak strikes their primary target inflicting 582757 Physical damage and 194253 Shadow damage.  
        This empowers the caster causing melee attacks to additionally absorb 33121 healing. This effect stacks until Rakfang strikes a new target, resetting to a single stack.
        
        -   [Impale](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265152/impale?dd=16) – Ezzorak slams targets within a 35 yard rear cone bleeding for 155402 Physical damage plus an additional 38850 Physical damage every 1 sec and stunning for 3 sec.  
            This occurs immediately after casting Rakfang.
            
        
    
-   ![Deadly](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-deadly.png "Deadly")![Important](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-important.png "Important")[Midnight Flames](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249748/midnight-flames?dd=16) – Upon reaching 100 energy Ezzorak and Vaelgor fly as one each spewing 194253 Shadow damage to all players plus an additional 17483 Shadow damage every 0.5 sec for 25 sec.

    -   ![Mythic](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-mythic.png "Mythic")[Cosmosis](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1263623/cosmosis?dd=16) – Xal'atath draws shadow power from her target creating an Astral Reflection. This extradimensional clone lingers momentarily casting the target's ability, then disintegrates.
        
    

**Lightbound Vanguard**

If present War Chaplain Senn, General Amias Bellamy, and/or Commander Venel Lightblood join in the battle against Vaelgor & Ezzorak.

-   ![Important](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-important.png "Important")[Radiant Barrier](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1248847/radiant-barrier?dd=16) – Senn eminates holy power for 9 sec blessing players within 0 yards. Every 1 sec this applies protection to nearby players that absorbs 1391065 Midnight Flames damage, divided evenly amongst players.  
    Additionally, gaining Radiant Barrier consumes Midnight Manifestation, spawning a Manifestation of Midnight or infusing an existing Manifestation for each aura consumed.  
    This barrier's effectiveness is limited to 75% of each damage occurrence.  
    If present, Bellamy and/or Lightblood increase the amount of absorb granted.
    
-   [Aura of Light](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1272867/aura-of-light?dd=16) – The presence of the Lightbound Vanguard weakens this enemy's vitality, reducing their maximum health by 5% and damage done by 5%.  
    This effect stacks for each present member of the vanguard.
