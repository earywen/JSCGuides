-   **Overview** – Vorasius creates Void Crystal barriers when slamming down his largest arms with [\[Smashing Frenzy\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244012/smashing-frenzy?dd=15). These barriers can be damaged by exploding Blistercreeps which trigger [\[Blisterburst\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1259184/blisterburst?dd=15) when killed.  
    Vorasius slowly sweeps the area with [\[Void Breath\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1241864/void-breath?dd=15) from one side to the other starting from a random side.
    
    -   ![DPS](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-dps.png "DPS")**Damage Dealers** –  
        Blistercreeps explode when killed and can damage Void Crystals with [\[Blisterburst\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1259184/blisterburst?dd=15).  
        [\[Aftershock\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1273067/aftershock?dd=15) strikes multiple times in rings expanding outward after [\[Smashing Frenzy\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244012/smashing-frenzy?dd=15).
        
    
    -   ![Tank](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-tank.png "Tank")**Tanks** –  
        If Vorasius strikes no targets directly with [\[Smashing Frenzy\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244012/smashing-frenzy?dd=15) it inflicts high damage to all enemies. Being hit by his largest arms inflicts [\[Smashed\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1241844/smashed?dd=15).  
        Vorasius uses [\[Overpowering Pulse\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244419/overpowering-pulse?dd=15) when no enemies are in melee range.
        
    
-   ![Deadly](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-deadly.png "Deadly")[Void Breath](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1256855/void-breath?dd=15) – A powerful beam of void energy inflicts 2354874 Shadow damage initially and 294359 Shadow damage to targets within its reach every 1 sec. and 8831 Shadow damage every 0.5 sec to all targets for 15 sec.
    
-   [Parasite Expulsion](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1254199/parasite-expulsion?dd=15) – Vorasius casts off parasitic creeps, scattering them about the nearby area every 2 sec for 4 sec along with excess void shards inflicting 206052 Shadow damage.
    
    -   [Blistercreep](https://www.wowhead.com/npc=255418/blistercreep) –
        
        In Mythic difficulty, is immune to crowd control effects.
        
        -   ![Important](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-important.png "Important")![Magic](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-magic.png "Magic")[Blisterburst](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1259186/blisterburst?dd=15) – A Blistercreep explodes on death inflicting 264923 Shadow damage to enemies and Void Crystals within 8 yards and causes all enemies within 8 yards to take 50% increased Shadow damage for 30 sec. Also inflicts 88308 Shadow damage to all enemies.
            
        -   [Creep Impact](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1272683/creep-impact?dd=15) – A falling Blistercreep Inflicts 412103 Shadow damage to enemies within 10 yards on impact.
            
        -   [Creepspatter](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1272527/creepspatter?dd=15) – A Blistercreep inflicts 206052 Shadow damage to enemies in nearby areas.
            
        
    
-   [Smashing Frenzy](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1241692/smashing-frenzy?dd=15) – Vorasius slams a giant claw into the ground applying Smashed and inflicting 588719 Shadowstrike damage to targets in the impact area and 147180 Shadowstrike damage to all targets. If no targets are present in the impact area the impact inflicts 588719 Shadowstrike damage to all targets instead.
    
    -   ![Tank](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-tank.png "Tank")[Smashed](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1241844/smashed?dd=15) – Incoming Physical damage increased by 150% for . This effect stacks.
        
    

-   [Aftershock](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1273067/aftershock?dd=15) – Smashing Frenzy and Crystaline Eruption create a series of aftershocks inflicting 412103 Physical damage in rings around the point of impact.
    
-   [Primordial Roar](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1260052/primordial-roar?dd=15) – A deep breath pulls enemies closer before a deafening roar knocks back and inflicts 206052 Sonic damage to all enemies.
    
    -   [Gathering Void](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1272937/gathering-void?dd=15) – Vorasius gathers void energy with every roar, inflicting 8831 Shadow damage per stack of this effect every 2 sec.
        
    
-   [Overpowering Pulse](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1244419/overpowering-pulse?dd=15) – Vorasius inflicts 88308 Shadow damage to all targets while not engaged in melee combat every 2 sec.
