-   **Overview** – Fallen-King Salhadaar siphons power from the surrounding machinery, slowly increasing his energy over time. Upon reaching 100 energy, he is overwhelmed by the Void and begins his [\[Cosmic Unraveling\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1246175/cosmic-unraveling?dd=16).  
    Periodically, Salhadaar will draw orbs of Concentrated Void from the machine toward him. When an orb of Concentrated Void reaches him, he performs a [\[Reckless Infusion\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1245960/reckless-infusion?dd=16).
