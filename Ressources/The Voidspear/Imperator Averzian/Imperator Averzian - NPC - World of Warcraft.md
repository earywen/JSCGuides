-   **Overview** – Imperator Averzian uses [\[Shadow's Advance\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1251361/shadows-advance?dd=15) to claim territory on the battlefield. Once a space is [\[Void Claimed\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1253918/void-claimed?dd=15) it cannot be retaken, and if Averzian claims three adjacent territories in a row he unleashes the [\[March of the Endless\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1251583/march-of-the-endless?dd=15) .  
    Causing an [\[Umbral Collapse\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249262/umbral-collapse?dd=15) on a territory being taken prevents its capture.  
    Dark Tears form over territories Averzian controls, allowing a portian of his forces to come through. These tears grow larger as the battle progresses, summoning stronger foes to enter the field.
    
    -   ![DPS](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-dps.png "DPS")**Damage Dealers** –  
        Abyssal Voidshapers attempt to use [\[Gathering Darkness\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1255749/gathering-darkness?dd=15) at full power.  
        Shadowguard Stalwarts attempt to empowertheir allies with [\[Pitch Bulwark\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1255702/pitch-bulwark?dd=15).
        
    -   ![Healer](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-healer.png "Healer")**Healers** –  
        [\[Dark Upheaval\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249251/dark-upheaval?dd=15) inflicts periodic damage to all players.  
        [\[Umbral Collapse\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249262/umbral-collapse?dd=15) inflicts damage to everyone that is reduced by the number of players at the impact location.
        
    -   ![Tank](https://wow.zamimg.com/images/icons/ej-tank.png "Tank")**Tanks** –  
        Averzian gains [\[Void Claimed\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1253918/void-claimed?dd=15) while close to his Dark Tears.  
        When Averzian strikes his current target he infects them with [\[Blackening Wounds\]](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265540/blackening-wounds?dd=15).
