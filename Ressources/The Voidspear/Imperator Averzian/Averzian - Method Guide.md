# Imperator Averzian  Heroic Boss Guide

![Imperator Averzian](https://www.method.gg/images/raidrace/bosses/the-voidspire/imperatoraverzian.png)

[Heroic](https://www.method.gg/guides/the-voidspire/imperator-averzian-heroic) Mythic

Purke

**Last Updated:** 28th Feb, 2026

## Raid Setup

![tank icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/tank-icon.png)

2 Tanks

![healer icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/healer-icon.png)

4 Healers

![dps icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/dps-icon.png)

14 DPS

## Introduction

Welcome to our Heroic Imperator Averzian guide for the first boss in The Voidspire Raid. This guide covers the fight mechanics and the strategy we recommend to defeat this boss on Heroic difficulty.

This boss is basically tic tac toe, which is perfect, because I used to play this all the time with my grandma while we were waiting for dinner to be done. This is my moment. Years of training for the most important raid mechanic of all time: not letting my opponent get three in a row.

**Fight Style:** Heavy Cleave fight

**Recommended Setup:** 2x Tank / 4x Healer / 14x DPS

**Required utilities:** None

**Hero/Lust usage:** On Pull

## Method Boss Review

This boss is a really nice take on what a first boss should be. It has a unique mechanic that we haven’t really seen before, without turning it into something annoying or overcomplicated.

Most first bosses are usually a bit of a letdown because they’re light on mechanics and don’t feel very special, so this is a welcome change.

The only real downside is that once you understand the tic tac toe game, there isn’t a whole lot else going on.

**Method Boss Rating:** (3/5)

## Easy Mode

This boss is tic tac toe. Do not let the boss claim three tiles in a line.

**Shadow’s Advance Board Phase:**

- Boss starts with [~~~~Shadow's Advance](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1251361/shadows-advance?dd=15) and spawns **3 Abyssal Voidshapers**.
- Each Voidshaper casts [~~~~Gathering Darkness](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1255749/gathering-darkness?dd=15). If it completes, that tile is claimed.

**Umbral Collapse saves 2 tiles:**

- You get **two [~~~~Umbral Collapse](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249262/umbral-collapse?dd=15)** soaks back-to-back.
- Raid leader calls which two tiles to save.
- [~~~~Umbral Collapse](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249262/umbral-collapse?dd=15) target runs to the called marker, everyone stacks to soak.
- As soon as the first soak explodes, sprint to the second tile and soak again.
- If done right, only **1 tile** gets claimed each wave.

**Portals after the Claims:**

- The surviving Voidshaper casts [~~~~Light's End](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1284699/lights-end?dd=15) and spawns a portal that shoots beams in 5 directions.
- Do not stand in the beams.
- This gets worse later because more claimed tiles means more portals.

**Add Priority:**

- Kill **Abyssal Voidshaper** first. If it hits 100 energy it becomes an **Obsidian Endwalker.**
- Cleave **Voidmaws** and the **Shadowguard Stalwart / Annihilators**.
- Kill Voidmaws before they reach a portal at sub 35% HP.
- Interrupt [~~~~Pitch Bulwark](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1255702/pitch-bulwark?dd=15) from the Stalwart/Annihilator or everything gets a massive absorb shield.

**Oblivion Soaks:**

- Every claimed tile spawns an **Oblivion** circle.
- Exactly **1 player** soaks each circle.
- If nobody soaks, the whole raid takes heavy damage.
- More claimed tiles is more circles, meaning it stops being free.

**Dodging:**

- [~~~~Void Fall](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1258883/void-fall?dd=15) is a knockback and lots of swirlies. Dodge.
- [~~~~Oblivion's Wrath](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1260712/oblivions-wrath?dd=15) is spikes that fire in a direction. Dodge.

**Tanks:**

- Swap on [~~~~Blackening Wounds](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1265540/blackening-wounds?dd=15) around **8-10 stacks** depending on comfort.

## Mechanics

Expand for details

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1253918/imperators-glory?dd=15)![Imperator’s Glory Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/achievement_boss_triumvirate_voidbrokenbrute.jpg)

### Imperator’s Glory

If the boss is standing within 10 yards of a claimed spot (a spot that was not destroyed with [~~~~Umbral Collapse](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249262/umbral-collapse?dd=15)), he gains:

- 75% increased damage done
- 90% reduced damage taken

Don’t let him hang out next to claimed tiles unless you want the world’s slowest pull.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1251361/shadows-advance?dd=15)![Shadow's Advance Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_shadow_antishadow.jpg)

### Shadow's Advance

The boss spawns minions and targets **three spaces**, each one spawning an **Abyssal Voidshaper**.

Voidshapers cast [~~~~Void Rupture](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1262036/void-rupture?dd=15). If the cast completes, they claim the spot. Using [~~~~Umbral Collapse](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249262/umbral-collapse?dd=15), you can destroy **two out of the three** Voidshapers and only have to fully fight one.

Voidshapers also have an energy bar. At 100 energy, they turn into an **Obsidian Endwalker**.

![Shadowguard Stalwart / Annihilator Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_arcane_arcaneresilience.jpg)

### Shadowguard Stalwart / Annihilator

An add that casts [~~~~Pitch Bulwark](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1255702/pitch-bulwark?dd=15), placing a large absorb barrier on its allies. This should be interrupted.

![Voidmaws Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_cosmicvoid_buff.jpg)

### Voidmaws

Small adds that give a stacking shadow DoT with each melee hit, and once they reach 35% HP, they start moving towards the nearest portal to heal up to full. During that, their movement speed is reduced by 75%.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249262/umbral-collapse?dd=15)![Umbral Collapse Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_arcane_arcanetorrent.jpg)

### Umbral Collapse

A random player gets a soak circle that explodes after a few seconds. Damage is reduced based on how many players are soaking.

If the circle is placed on top of an **Abyssal Voidshaper**, it kills the add and prevents that spot from being claimed.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1265540/blackening-wounds?dd=15)![Blackening Wounds Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_arcane_arcanepotency_nightborne.jpg)

### Blackening Wounds

![tank icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/tank-icon.png)

Each hit reduces the Tank’s max HP by 4% for 20 seconds. Swap once the stacks make the tank too squishy to comfortably survive (at around **8-10 stacks**).

![Oblivion Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv12_ability_priest_voidvolley.jpg)

### Oblivion

All claimed spaces spawn a circle that must be soaked by one player.

Soaking deals a hit plus a DoT for several seconds. If nobody soaks a circle, the entire raid takes heavy damage instead.

As the fight goes on and more spaces get claimed, this gets more and more annoying.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1249251/dark-upheaval?dd=15)![Dark Upheaval Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/ability_priest_voidentropy.jpg)

### Dark Upheaval

![healer icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/role/healer-icon.png)

Unavoidable raid-wide damage throughout the fight.

![Light's End Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/ability_creature_cursed_03.jpg)

### Light's End

The remaining **Abyssal Voidshaper** that successfully claimed its spot spawns a portal and emits beams in five directions, damaging anyone caught in the lines.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1260712/oblivions-wrath?dd=15)![Oblivion's Wrath Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/spell_priest_void-blast.jpg)

### Oblivion's Wrath

The boss spawns spikes around him that, after a short delay, fire in the direction they’re facing, dealing damage to anyone hit.

[~~~~](https://www.wowhead.com/spell=1258883/void-fall?dd=15)![Void Fall Mechanic Icon](https://www.method.gg/images/icons/mechanics/inv_nullstone_void.jpg)

### Void Fall

Knocks all players away and spawns a large number of circles on the ground that must be dodged. Standard “don’t stand in it” mechanic.

## Strategy

The Imperator Averzian Heroic fight is basically playing tic tac toe, and your goal is to stop the boss from winning. That’s the whole fight. Everything revolves around that board.

### The Tic Tac Toe Mini Game

The boss opens with [~~~~Shadow's Advance](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1251361/shadows-advance?dd=15), spawning **three Abyssal Voidshapers**. They start casting [~~~~Gathering Darkness](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1255749/gathering-darkness?dd=15). If that cast completes, the space is claimed, think of it like the boss placing an X.

Here’s the map layout:

![imperator averzian markers](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/2802090-imperator-averzian-markers.jpg)

If the boss ever manages to claim **three fields in a line**, it triggers an enrage mechanic that quickly wipes the raid. So your entire goal is to make sure the board never reaches that point.

### Umbral Collapse Assignments

The good news is you can destroy **two out of the three** Voidshapers each wave using [~~~~Umbral Collapse](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249262/umbral-collapse?dd=15).

When you get targeted by [~~~~Umbral Collapse](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1249262/umbral-collapse?dd=15), move to the marker your raid leader calls, and everyone else soaks with you to split the damage.

This happens back-to-back:

- So you soak the first one
- Then you immediately run to the second one and soak again

![imperator averzian soak example](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/2802089-imperator-averzian-soak-example.jpg)

If you do it correctly, only **one** of the three spots will be claimed each set.

Next time [~~~~Shadow's Advance](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1251361/shadows-advance?dd=15) happens, your raid leader needs to call on the fly which two spots to soak based on what’s already claimed. Thinking ahead matters a lot here, because one bad choice can set up a “free line” later.

### Portals and Light’s End

After the two collapses, the remaining Voidshaper will cast Light's End, spawning a portal that fires beams in five directions. Don’t stand in the lines.

This gets more annoying later, because as more spots get claimed, you’ll have more portals active.

![imperator averzian portals](https://www.method.gg/images/uploads/guides/2802099-imperator-averzian-portals.jpg)

Always make sure to keep the boss and the adds away from the claimed spots (portals).

### Add Priority

After the board part, you’re fighting:

- **Abyssal Voidshaper** (priority kill)
- **Shadowguard Stalwart**
- **Voidbound Annihilator**
- A bunch of **Voidmaw** adds

Abyssal Voidshaper is always the kill priority. You want it dead before it reaches 100 energy, otherwise it becomes an **Obsidian Endwalker**, and that’s basically the “why did we let this happen” moment.

Stalwart / Anihilators and the Voidmaws can be cleaved down, but you must interrupt [~~~~Pitch Bulwark](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1255702/pitch-bulwark?dd=15) from the Stalwart and the Annihilator, because if it goes through it puts a huge absorb shield on everything and makes the wave drag out for no reason.

Voidmaws will start to move towards a portal once below 35% and if they reach it, they heal up to full, but this should rarely be an issue as they die fairly quickly.

### Oblivion Soaks

Each claimed spot also spawns a soak mechanic called **Oblivion**.

Each circle needs exactly one player soaking it. If nobody soaks, the entire raid takes heavy damage instead.

Early on this looks like a joke because there’s only one circle. Later, once several tiles are claimed, you suddenly have multiple circles going off and it becomes a real assignment mechanic.

### Dodge Mechanics

[~~~~Void Fall](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1258883/void-fall?dd=15) and [~~~~Oblivion's Wrath](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1260712/oblivions-wrath?dd=15) happen regularly. They’re just spikes and swirlies that you dodge. Nothing fancy, just don’t get clipped.

### Tank Notes

Tanks need to watch [~~~~Blackening Wounds](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/spell=1265540/blackening-wounds?dd=15). Once stacks get high and the max HP reduction ramps up, random mechanics can suddenly become lethal.

Swap at around **8-10 stacks**, depending on comfort and healing.

As long as you handle the tic tac toe calls cleanly and kill the adds quickly, you shouldn’t have any problem defeating this boss.
