**Belo'ren, Child of Al'ar** is a level ?? [boss](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Boss "Boss") in the [March on Quel'Danas](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/wiki/March_on_Quel%27Danas "March on Quel'Danas").

## Adventure Guide

_One of the last clutch of [Al'ar](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Al%27ar "Al'ar"), Belo'ren was raised for the purpose of protecting the [Isle of Quel'Danas](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Isle_of_Quel%27Danas "Isle of Quel'Danas"). In the aftermath of the [Voidspire](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Voidspire "Voidspire"). Belo'ren has been irradiated by [Void](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Void "Void"). Confused and freshly hatched, the phoenix is focused on its sole task: Keep any invaders from reaching the [Sunwell](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Sunwell "Sunwell")._

## Overview

Belo'ren guards the Sunwell with both Light and Void magic. His **Voidlight Convergence** infuses players with either Light or Void, protecting them from one while leaving them vulnerable to the other.

When his health is depleted, Belo'ren retreats into an ashen egg and begins to cast **Rebirth**. Players must damage Belo'ren to diminish his **Immortal Flame** before he is reborn anew.

-   **Light Feather** reduces incoming Holy damage. **Void Feather** reduces incoming Shadow damage.
-   **Light Burn** and **Void Burn** absorb significant healing and inflict damage over time until fully healed.

###  [![Tank Alert](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/images/thumb/Tank-icon-MN.png/20px-Tank-icon-MN.png?af9532)](https://warcraft.wiki.gg/wiki/Adventure_Guide#Alerts "Tank Alert") Tanks

-   **Light Feather** reduces incoming Holy damage. **Void Feather** reduces incoming Shadow damage.
-   Failing to hit a player with **Guardian's Edict** empowers Belo'ren, significantly increasing his damage.

## Abilities

### Stage One: Phoenix Reborn

Belo'ren ascends in his majestic phoenix form wielding the power of the radiant Light and devouring Void.

### Stage Two: Ashen Shell

As Belo'ren falls he retreats into an ashen egg and kindles the fire of rebirth.
